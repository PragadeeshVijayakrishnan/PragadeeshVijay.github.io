About Me

I’m Pragadeesh — Cinema and music have always been at the center of what I care about — they shape how I think, feel, and express myself. Directors like Yorgos Lanthimos, Tarantino, the Safdie brothers, Paul Thomas Anderson, Wong Kar Wai, and Scorsese have deeply influenced my taste and the way I look at storytelling — whether it’s through mood, chaos, or silence. I’m about to begin my journey at CIT as a Computer Science student, and I’ve recently started getting into coding — curious about how it can open up new creative spaces. I’m also working on personal short films, trying to capture the thoughts and feelings I can’t always put into words..
